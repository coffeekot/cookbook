Hot Milk Cake

A very easy sponge cake that would be consumed in a matter of seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cup flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder (no heap)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cup sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Powdered sugar (garnish, optional)

Directions

  1. Pre-heat the oven 350 F. Grease 9x13" pan or alternatively line it with parchment paper.
  2. Heat whole milk with butter until bubbling and butter is melted, about 5 minutes.
  3. Mix flour, baking powder and salt in a separate bowl.
  4. In another bowl, mix sugar and eggs until fluffy, about 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add flour mix to egg mix, mix on low until just incorporated.
  6. Still mixing, add hot butter-milk mixture and vanilla extract, stir until combined.
  7. Pour batter into the baking pan, bake 350 F about 30 minutes until lightly browned on the outside.
  8. Let cool completely on a rack and dust with powdered sugar before serving.
