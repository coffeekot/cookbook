Cucumber Spicy Salad

Another easy spicy Korean salad!

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers, as much as you can eat. Adjust other ingredients accordingly (by heart).
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp gochugaru
  • 1 tbsp chopped scallion
  • 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Directions

  1. Cut cucumbers into thin, round slices.
  2. Toss with 1 tsp salt and set aside for 15 minutes for liquid to drain.
  3. Drain liquid. Add the rest of the ingredients.
  4. Mix and serve!

Cucumber salad will get better the longer it stands in the fridge.
