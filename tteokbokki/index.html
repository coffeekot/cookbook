Tteokbokki

Popular Korean street food is within your grasp with the right ingredients stockpiled from H-mart.

Ingredients

Base

  • A handful of frozen rice cakes, maybe 1 cup. I do as much as I can eat.

Sauce

  • About 2/3 cup of water
  • 1 tbsp gochujang
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp gochugaru
  • 1 tsp garlic (fresh or powder, optional)
  • 1-2 tbsp sugar
  • Cheese cubes (any cheese will do, I use babybel. Optional)

Garnish

  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • A sprinkle of sesame seeds
  • A sprinkle of chopped green onions

Directions

  1. Add all sauce ingredients to a pan without the cheese on medium to high heat.
  2. Add rice cakes.
  3. Stir until the sauce is almost desired consistency and rice cakes seem fairly cooked through. About 5-7 min.
  4. Lower the heat, add cheese and cover for additional 3 minutes for cheese to melt.
  5. Dump into a bowl.
  6. Add garnish and enjoy immediately.
