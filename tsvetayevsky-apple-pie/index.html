Tsvetayevsky Apple Pie

Need to get rid of apples? Just bake this great pie.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup butter, room temperature
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda bubbled with vinegar

Cream filling

  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 tbsp flour

Filling

  • About 5 apples

Directions

  1. Mix all dough ingredients.
  2. Create a pie border in a round tin 9 to 11 inches in diameter.
  3. Clean and thinly slice the apples, add them to the tin.
  4. Mix all cream ingredients, pour over apples.
  5. Bake 355 F, 50 minutes.
