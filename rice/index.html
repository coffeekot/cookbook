Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 3 cups of water
  • salt to taste (1 tbsp)

Directions

  1. Wash rice until water is not as cloudy anymore.
  2. Get to boiling on medium to high heat.
  3. Add salt.
  4. Cover, let cook on low heat for 20 minutes until there’s no more water on the bottom. DON’T MIX!
  5. Turn the heat off, let it be covered for 10 minutes.
